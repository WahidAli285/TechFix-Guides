How to Fix Browser Showing Blank Page

A blank page in your browser usually occurs due to corrupted cache, incompatible extensions, outdated browsers, incorrect settings, or network issues. To fix it, clear cache and cookies, disable extensions, update your browser, reset settings, or troubleshoot connectivity problems.

‚ùì Why Does My Browser Show a Blank Page?

A blank page can appear when a browser fails to load content. Causes include temporary file corruption, slow or unstable internet, problematic extensions, misconfigured settings, or even malware. Understanding the reason helps apply the right solution.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why do some pages load blank while others work?

This may happen due to specific scripts on certain websites, extension conflicts, or cached data. Clearing cache and disabling extensions usually solves it.

Will resetting my browser delete bookmarks?

No, bookmarks and saved passwords remain intact. Resetting only restores default settings and disables extensions temporarily.

Can slow internet cause blank pages?

Yes, slow or intermittent connections may prevent the browser from loading content fully, resulting in blank pages.
